Event Description

This event tests your skills in designing and modelling. Show your imagination and creativity at designing to become the ultimate CHAMPION.

Prizes worth INR 6000

Rules:

1. The event will have 3 rounds

First Round - MCQ round based on SOLIDWORKS software.

Second Round - Drawing the missing parts of given assembly and do the complete assembly.

Third Round – A real life problem will be given and one has to use their creativity to design an innovative product.

2. Round 1 is a qualifying round to be able to compete in the second round.

3. 10 teams will qualify for the second round. The final result will be a cumulative score of the second and third rounds.

4. A team can consist of maximum 2 participants who may be from different colleges.

5. The decision of the judges and event heads shall be considered final and binding to all. Any participants found misbehaving will be immediately disqualified.

6. Only UG students are allowed to participate carrying original, valid college ID and registration receipt of the event.

7. Participants should be present 30 minutes prior to the commencement of event.

8. The rules may be changed before the commencement of the event based on the discretion of the organisers.
