Event Description

Do you think logically? Then prove it .Do you always think in procedural manner? How much you are familiar with microcontroller? Are you able to express your thoughts into codes?

If you can talk to microcontroller, you just have the passion for embedded systems which needs to be unleashed; we give you the right platform.

Prizes worth INR 6000

Rules:

Team Members Limit: 2

There will be 3 rounds:

ROUND 1 –

• All the teams have to first pass the hurdle of prelims.

• It will be a written test of simple multiple choice questions to assess participant's general aptitude and basic electronics knowledge.

• There will be 40 MCQs to be solved in 30 mins.

ROUND 2 –

Qualified teams will be participating in an interactive session and a small quiz.

ROUND 3 –

• The top teams from Round 2 will advance to this round.

• Before this round starts, a brief description of PIC microcontroller and MP- LAB IDE will be given.

• Problem statement will be given and finalists have to implement the code on the given Kit. (Kit will be provided.)

• Solution for problem statement is expected in embedded C language.

• This round would be judged by experts.
