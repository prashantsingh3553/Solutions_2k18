Event Description

"Here is an event to challenge your numerical abilities and to display how much quick your awareness is .There is a clash between two armies and numbers are the essential for both to win the battle. Can you be the victorious team?? "

While reading newspapers, has it happened with you that you end up at the Sudoku section out of nowhere? Yes you got it right .Your appetite for Sudoku will be taken care of here with the right kind of technical clues.

INTRODUCTION:

The competition will feature a blank or partially filled Sudoku grid. Questions will be given and each question will point towards one or more coordinate on the puzzle.

FASTEST ONE TO SOLVE CORRECTLY WINS!!!

Competition Format:

Here will be preliminary rounds which and from which finalists teams will be selected on the basis of solving the Sudoku questions correctly and early submission of the answer.

PREREQUISITES:

1. Basic understanding and rules on how to solve Sudoku puzzles.

2. Basic aptitude, logical reasoning, numerical ability, mathematical questions.

Prizes worth INR 6000

Rules:

1) This is a team event.

2) There will be at max. 2 members in a team and team members can be from different colleges.

3) No calculators / any electronic devices are allowed.

4) If any of the above mentioned devices found, the team will be disqualified.

Round 1

1. Two Sudoku puzzles will be given to each team.

2. Time allotted will be 30 minutes.

3. Top 30 teams according to their timing of completion will qualify for the second round.

Round 2

1. Two Sudoku puzzles will be given to each team.

2. Numbers in the Sudoku will be assigned with suitable alphabets whose value will be attained after solving the respective aptitude questions.

3. Sudoku will be only evaluated if all the answers for the aptitude questions are correct

INSTRUCTIONS FOR PARTICIPANTS:

All participants are required to bring their college i-card and registration receipt along with them.

All the participants are required to report 15 mins prior to the contest.
