Event Description

1) Abstract should be submitted before 16 jan 2018.

2) Problem statements for this competition are the problems faced in AIT .

3) Any problem statement which is related to India is accepted.

4) Round 1is abstract submission and Round 2 is presentation round (idea presentation).

5) For round 1 abstract should be in the following format.

a) Problem Statement which you are addressing.

b) Solution to that problem statement.

c) Detailed cost to implement the solution.

d) Impact of the solution

e) Advantages & Disadvantages of the solution.Mail the Abstract to solutionsait04@gmail.com (Mention all the team members name, contact number, Reg number).

6) Ten teams will be shortlisted for the second round.

7) Second round would Idea presentation round, Idea should be presented with the help of a ppt.(All teams will have to present their ppt).

8) Maximum Time for PPT presentation is 10+ 3 min, presentation followed by Q&A round.

9) Team limit 4 Members.

10) Prize money worth Rs 10,000/-

RAMANUJAN

There will be two rounds

ROUND 1 :

• Total time : 45 minutes

• Total questions : 30

• Consists of two sections

SECTION 1 contains 20 MCQs each carrying 3 marks.( -1 for each wrong attempt)

SECTION 2 contains 10 subjective questions each carrying 4 marks. There is no negative marking for section 2.

15 teams will be shortlisted for round 2.

ROUND 2 :

1. Total time : 1 hour

2. Total tasks : 5 (Will be disclosed then and there)

3. Each task has points depending on the level of difficulty.

4. Top three teams will be awarded finally and will be given certificate of appreciation.

Prizes worth INR 10000

3. Maximum of two in a team.

4. No calculators / any electronic devices are allowed.

5. If any of the above mentioned devices found, the team will be disqualified.

8. Participants must present their registration receipts at the reporting desk.

9. The decision of judges and organizers will be final and binding.

10. The coordinators reserve the right to change the rule at any point of time before the commencement of the event.
