Event Description

Excelsior, meaning ever upwards is a competition of your innovation and a correct execution of that very innovation done in an analytical and logical manner. Do you have what it takes to present your paper in front of our highly knowledgeable judges and impress them to take it home? Come and weigh in your chance at Excelsior, where you strive to be the best amongst all.

Prizes worth INR 10000

Rules:

1. Two rounds to be held.

2. Maximum 2 members per team.

3. There is no specific topic for the papers.

4. The paper must be STRICTLY in IEEE format. Only text directly related to the item being presented and any accompanying illustrations, tables, figures, etc., will count toward the 6-page limit. Title pages, tables of contents, and bibliographies shall not be counted toward the limit.

5. All papers should have a bibliography/works-cited page and appropriate footnotes (any format, such as MLA, APA, etc., as long as its use is consistent throughout the document, will be acceptable). Papers omitting a bibliography or using plagiarized material will be automatically disqualified.

6. Photographs should be treated as being equivalent to figures, with the caption being placed at the bottom of the photograph. Photocopies of photographs are not acceptable.

7. The contestants are required to send only a soft copy of their entry along with an abstract (not more than 1000 words) to the following mail id: solutionsait04@gmail.com

8. Presentations may be accompanied by visual aids.

ROUND I: ABSTRACT SUBMISSION

Submit your abstracts before the scheduled date of deadline that is 16 jan 2018.

Check the result to know if your team has qualified or not.

ROUND II: PRESENTATION

•10 entries to be shortlisted for the finals.

•Teams will get 8-10 minutes to present their presentation

•Teams will be penalized in case they exceed the time limit

•Question/Answer round by the judges and audience will approximately be for 5 minutes.

• Not more than 15 minutes will be spent on each team
