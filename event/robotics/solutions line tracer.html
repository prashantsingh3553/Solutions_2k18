Event Description

No of team members: maximum 4

1) Problem statement:

Make an autonomous robot with a team of max 4 members, to complete the given track, following a line with minimum time and violations.

2) Arena specification:

A] The arena will consists of black line on white background. The thickness of black line is 3 cm.

B] Black line consists of curves, angular bends, random shapes & loops.

C] Material for calibration will be provided.

3) Robot specifications:

A] Robot should fit in square of 20cm*20cm. No limit on height.

B] Robot must have a working & easily accessible ON/OFF switch. Connecting and disconnecting the battery for this function is unacceptable.

C] Robot should be powered by battery not more than 12 V. No power supply will be provided. Bots with their own power supply will be given preferences.

D] Robot must operate without inputs from the operator.

E] Robot must not damage the arena or pose threat to the spectators in any way. If the organizers feel so, the team will be disqualified.

F] Computer system will be not provided but the participants should preferably bring their own computer system (laptop) and software required.

H] The bot should be stable and must stand on its own.

Prizes worth INR 6000

Rules:

A] Competition consists of 2 rounds i.e. eliminations & final round.

B] Each team can consist of maximum 4 persons.

C]1st Round:Robot has to complete the track with minimum time and minimum violations.

2nd Round:Selected teams from 1st round will complete in 2nd round. Track point of track are different. Modified rules will be declared on the spot or previous night.

D] For each hand touch, the participant will have to start from the previous checkpoint or the point of contact. Also, hand touch will incur a penalty of 10 sec .

E] The scoring will be time based and there are some points reserved for explaining your bots design, its specifications and algorithms implemented in it .The Total score will be the addition of the two’s.

F] Decision of organizers will be final & abiding to all.
