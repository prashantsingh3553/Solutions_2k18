●   RULES AND SPECIFICATION :-

1. Participants should report at least 30 mins before the event , the students which report after the scheduled time will be disqualified.

2. For junior level students of 8 & 9 will compete and for senior level 10 &11 students.

3. Individual participation.

4. No calculators / any electronic devices are allowed.

5. If any of the above mentioned devices found, the student will be disqualified.

6. Mobile phones should be switched off during the conduct of the event.

7. Participants must carry their valid school identity cards.

8. Participants must present their registration receipts at the reporting desk.

9. The decision of judges and organizers will be final and binding.
